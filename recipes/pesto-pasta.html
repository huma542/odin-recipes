Easy Pesto Pasta

Description

A vibrant and quick meal using fresh basil pesto, garlic, and al dente pasta. Perfect for busy weeknights.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz spaghetti or fusilli
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil pesto
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsp pine nuts (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.

  2. Cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente.

  3. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water, then drain the rest.

  4. the pasta with pesto and reserved water until creamy.

  5. Top with Parmesan and pine nuts before serving.
