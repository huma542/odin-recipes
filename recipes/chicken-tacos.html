Zesty Chicken Tacos

Description

Fresh, vibrant, and packed with flavor, these street-style chicken tacos are topped with onions, cilantro, and a squeeze of fresh lime juice.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken breast or thighs, diced
  • 2 tbsp taco seasoning (cumin, chili powder, garlic powder)
  • Small corn or flour tortillas
  • 1/2 white onion, finely diced
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 2 limes, cut into wedges
  • Optional: Avocado, salsa, or sour cream

Steps

  1. a tablespoon of oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.

  2. Add the chicken and cook for 8-10 minutes until golden brown and cooked through.

  3. While the chicken cooks, warm your tortillas in a dry pan or over a gas flame.

  4. Assemble the tacos by placing chicken in the center of the tortillas.

  5. Top with onions and cilantro.

  6. Serve immediately with lime wedges on the side for squeezing.
