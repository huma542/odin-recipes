Crispy Smash Burgers

Description

The secret to a great smash burger is a high-heat sear that creates a crispy, caramelized crust on the beef patty.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef (80/20 blend)
  • 4 brioche buns
  • 4 slices of American cheese
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Sliced pickles and onions

Steps

  1. Divide the beef into 4 loosely packed balls. Do not overwork the meat.

  2. Heat a cast-iron skillet over high heat until it starts to smoke slightly.

  3. Place a meat ball in the skillet and "smash" it flat with a heavy spatula.

  4. Season heavily with salt and pepper. Cook for 2 minutes until a dark crust forms.

  5. Flip the patty, add a slice of cheese, and cook for 1 more minute.

  6. Toast the buns and assemble with your favorite toppings.
