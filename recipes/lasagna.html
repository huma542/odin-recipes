Lasagna

Description

This is a hearty, traditional Italian lasagna made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, and creamy cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 box of lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 jar of marinara sauce
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella

Steps

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles in salted water until al dente.

  2. Brown the ground beef in a skillet and stir in the marinara sauce.

  3. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheeses in a baking dish.

  4. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30 minutes until bubbly.
