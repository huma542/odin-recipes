Moist Banana Bread

Description

This is a foolproof recipe for the most moist and flavorful banana bread you will ever taste. It is perfect for using up those overripe bananas sitting on your counter!

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1.5 cups all-purpose flour
  • A pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 4x8 inch loaf pan.

  2. In a large bowl, mash the ripe bananas until smooth.

  3. Stir the melted butter into the mashed bananas.

  4. Mix in the baking soda, salt, sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla extract.

  5. in the flour last, being careful not to over-mix.

  6. the batter into your prepared loaf pan.

  7. Bake for 50 to 60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
